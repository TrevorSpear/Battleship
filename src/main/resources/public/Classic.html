Welcome To Battleship!

To play, simply press the play button on one of the following game modes below!

Classic

Your standard battleship game. You get five ships to place. After placing them, you may start firing on your enemy's board
To win, sink your enemy's ships before they sink yours!

Redux

Somewhat similar to Classic but with the following changes:
The Dinghy and Clipper are civilian ships that replace the Cruiser and Destroyer and sink after only one hit
You may scan instead of firing. Doing so will tell you if there is a ship located in a one block radius ( a "+" formation) around where you select
Your battleship and submarine are able to evade scans
